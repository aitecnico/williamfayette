A tiny bit about me

Hi I’m William Fayette, a recent Software Engineering Bootcamp graduate. I am a problem solver by nature and inherently creative which has led me to develop a keen interest in the realm of software development. When the pandemic hit I was working as an AV Engineer installing computer servers, communication components, and Zoom rooms for various dot com establishments such as Google and Square in the Bay Area. As working from home became the new normal, I decided to finally go for it and pursue my life long dream of becoming a dev by enrolling at Springboard.
I have always been intrigued by technology. In my early years I worked on computers at my father's office and self-taught myself how to build them and take them apart. My love for technology evolved into a love for music too. I create music on Ableton Live, which is a software that allows you to compose music and loop sounds. I also nerd out on reverse engineering songs and enjoy performing live sets. I feel my skillset is highly valuable and I am especially interested in creating software that merges my two passions- coding and music.
I’ve learned a lot at Springboard, from frontend and backend development to databases, data structures, and algorithms. I enjoy writing code and keeping up with the latest trends and I am excited to build upon my foundation so I can further develop my coding skills, expand my networks, and create new projects.
